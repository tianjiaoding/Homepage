a post with typograms

Created in April 29, 2024

2024   ·   formatting   diagrams   ·   sample-posts

This is an example post with some typograms code.

```typograms
+----+
|    |---> My first diagram!
+----+
```

Which generates:

+----+
|    |---> My first diagram!
+----+

Another example:

```typograms
.------------------------.
|.----------------------.|
||"https://example.com" ||
|'----------------------'|
| ______________________ |
||                      ||
||   Welcome!           ||
||                      ||
||                      ||
||  .----------------.  ||
||  | username       |  ||
||  '----------------'  ||
||  .----------------.  ||
||  |"*******"       |  ||
||  '----------------'  ||
||                      ||
||  .----------------.  ||
||  |   "Sign-up"    |  ||
||  '----------------'  ||
||                      ||
|+----------------------+|
.------------------------.
```

which generates:
